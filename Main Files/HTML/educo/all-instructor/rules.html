Rules & Guidelines

  • home
  • >

1. Admission & Eligibility

  • Admission is finalized only upon full fee payment and original document submission.
  • Failure to produce documents may lead to cancellation.

2. Fee Payment

  • As per university guidelines
  • Freshers must pay full fees
  • Existing students may opt for two installments

3. Late Fees

  • Arts & Science: ₹20/day
  • Allied Health Sciences: ₹100/day

4. Hostel Fee Delay

  • Late fine: ₹50/day for both tuition and hostel fee

5. Dress Code

  • Students must adhere to prescribed dress code on campus.

6. Attendance

  • Medical leave beyond 3 days requires documentation

7. Library

  • Books must be returned within 7 days after post exams
  • Late return attracts fines
  • Lost books = replacement + ₹100 fee
  • Reference books are for in-library use only

8. Laboratory

  • Any damage to lab equipment must be compensated for
  • All practical work must be done under faculty supervision

9. Internal Assessments

  • Two internal exams per year
  • Failing in the internals leads to failure in the final exams

10. Sports

  • Participation in sports and physical education is encouraged
  • Selection handled by the Sports Officer

11. Transfer Certificate (TC)

  • TC requests must be signed by the Head of Institution
  • No-dues form must be submitted via the online portal
  • TC will be issued after the settlement of all dues
