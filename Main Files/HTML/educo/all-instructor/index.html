about us

Best Allied Health Science College in Tamilnadu

Cheran College of Allied Health Science, located in Coimbatore, is one of the top allied health science colleges in Tamilnadu. Run by the renowned Cheran Foundation Trust and affiliated with The Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, the college offers B.Sc. programs in Medical Laboratory Technology, Operation Theatre Technology, Radiography, Physician Assistant and Critical Care Technology.

With 33 years of educational excellence under the Cheran Group, our programs focus on clinical skills, hands-on training, and industry-relevant knowledge. Situated near Coimbatore's expanding healthcare hub, students gain strong clinical exposure and placement support.
